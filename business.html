1. Why Project Evan for Your Business?

Traditional social media and business directories treat discovery as a one-way broadcast. Project Evan is different. We're built around real human connection — not ad targeting.

Your customers use Project Evan because they genuinely want to discover activities, meet people, and build community. They're not doom-scrolling. When they see your business on Project Evan, they're actively looking for exactly what you offer.

  • Verified presence — Your listing is authenticated, giving customers confidence.
  • Community-first discovery — Users find you through their peers' recommendations, not algorithmic manipulation.
  • Privacy-friendly — Your customers' data isn't sold or tracked across the web. Trust builds stronger communities.
  • No algorithmic paywall — You don't need to "boost" content or pay per impression. Your authentic presence reaches people actively seeking you.

2. Business Verification

Trust is essential to offline discovery. Project Evan verifies that your business is legitimate through a transparent, straightforward process:

The Verification Process

  • Ownership Confirmation — We verify that you are authorised to represent this business through business registration records, tax documents, or direct communication with business officials.
  • Location & Details Accuracy — We confirm your physical address, contact information, and operating hours match public records.
  • Identity Check — Key business representatives complete identity verification to prevent impersonation.
  • Badging System — Verified businesses receive a visible "Verified" badge on their listing, so customers know they're discovering a legitimate, accountable business.

Once verified, your business is marked as authentic in the Project Evan community — a signal that customers trust and look for.
